Hydrocarbon tank aerial

Potential damages

The tank can be carried away by water. It becomes a dangerous floating object and its content spreads causing pollution of hydrocarbons. They can also attack a pipeline or even people. A plastic tank may deform if the pressure of the water develops. Water can also enter the tank through the vents.

Objectives of prevention works

Avoid the archimedes thrust on the tanks or secure it to a structure resistant to this thrust.

Works for the resiting strategy

1 - Place or move the tank in a non-submersible place.
2 - Or reinforce the support and anchorage of the tank.

Works for the yielding strategy

1 - Place or move the tank in a non-submersible place.
2 - Or reinforce the support and anchorage of the tank.
