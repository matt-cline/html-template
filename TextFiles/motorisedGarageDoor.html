Motorised garage door

Additional criterion

Water height > 1m (zone 3)

Potential damages

Electric drive motor: deterioration.

Objectives of prevention works

The electric motors and their controls must be disconnected from the water and their operation must be provided by a manual mechanical solution. Indeed, it may be necessary to close or open before systems repair or the return of electrical current to facilitate drying or prevent intrusions.

Works for the resiting strategy

No suggestion for resisting strategy.

Works for the yielding strategy

Prefer manual or electric disengageable controls.

Vulnerability from the security of people point of view

0 - No impact on personal safety.

Vulnerability from return to normal point of view

0 - No impact on return to normal.
