Vulnerability from the security of people point of view

1 - The ventilator may present a hazard like any electrical equipment. However, it is generally not very accessible, so the impact on the safety of people is low.

Vulnerability from return to normal point of view

1 - On one hand, ventilation can be done naturally by opening the windows. On the other hand, the replacement of the fan is a simple and fast operation. The impact on return to normal is low.
