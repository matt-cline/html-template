Presence of wood in the walls in elevation

Additional criterion

Duration of immersion > 2 days (for zone 3).

Potential damages

Deformation of wooden frames.
Deformation or degradation of wooden filling panels.

Objectives of prevention works

No suggestion for preventive work

Works for the resiting strategy

No suggesion for the resisting strategy.

Works for the yielding strategy

No suggestion for the yielding strategy.

Vulnerability from the security of people point of view

0 - Except in the exceptional case of cracks, lack of solidity or collapse, such potential damage does not affect the safety of persons.

Vulnerability from return to normal point of view

1 - In general, work on the rehabilitation of walls in elevation (mainly outside walls) does not affect the reintegration of the premises which is conditioned, first, by drying.
